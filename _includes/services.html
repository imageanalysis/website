What we do

Standardised MRI data analysis and cost-effective image-based study design in rheumatology, neorology and oncology.

Dynamika

We develop Dynamika, the leading cloud-based software for scoring of static and contrast-enhanced MRI. Dynamika provides a standardized workflow for reproducible, quantitative image analysis of MRI.

Image Acquisition

We support hospitals and clinical trials around the world define MRI sequences best suited to their needs. Our clients get the most out of their data when we get involved in MRI sequence design.

Contract Research
